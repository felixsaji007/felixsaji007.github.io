Mini Project-Butyl Rubber Dam [ 08/2019 – 01/2020 ]

By: Felix Saji

Rubber dams are inflatable and deflectable hydraulic structures. These are structures made of high strength fabric adhering with rubber, which forms a ballooned rubber bag when filled with water or air and anchored to the basement concrete floor, and are used for water retention. i. The project involves the study on the feasibility of various materials that could be used in construction of rubber dams. ii. The project includes introduction of a 2-channel rubber dam for irrigating selected lands and control of water (model).
